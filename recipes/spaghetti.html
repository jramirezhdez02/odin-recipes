Spaghetti

Description

Easy weeknight-friendly spaghetti and meat sauce made from scratch in under 40 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Check the package directions for the spaghetti you plan to cook
  2. Set your timer for 1 to 2 minutes less than the suggested cooking time
  3. Cook your spaghetti in salted water
  4. Toss your cooked spaghetti with your favorite sauce.
